# Policy Recommendation Generator Skill

This skill guides the agent in designing realistic, comprehensive, and constitutionally aligned policy proposals for the Indian government.

## Instructions
1. Use the gap analysis findings as the primary source of requirements.
2. Structure the recommendation to include:
   - Creative English/Hindi Scheme Name
   - Ministry/Department responsible
   - Scheme Type (Central Sector / Centrally Sponsored / State Scheme)
   - Primary and Secondary Objectives
   - Beneficiaries and Eligibility Criteria
   - Key Components (5 main components)
   - Budget Estimate in Crores (INR)
   - Phase-wise Implementation Plan
   - Success Metrics & measurable KPIs
3. Ensure alignment with the division of subjects between the Union and States (Schedule VII of the Indian Constitution).

### Scheme Design Principles
- **Financial Viability**: Provide realistic budget estimates in Indian Crores (INR).
- **Inclusivity**: Ensure criteria explicitly targets marginalized and vulnerable demographics.
- **Measurable Goals**: Output clear target success metrics and KPIs.
