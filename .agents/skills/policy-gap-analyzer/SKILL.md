# Policy Gap Analyzer Skill

This skill guides the agent in identifying policy gaps by analyzing user-provided sector issues and comparing them against existing Indian state and central government frameworks.

## Instructions
1. Review the target state/UT and sector/domain selected by the user.
2. Identify major existing schemes in that area (e.g., Ayushman Bharat for Health, Samagra Shiksha for Education).
3. Evaluate the user's specific problem statement against the provisions of those schemes.
4. Pinpoint missing elements, implementation bottlenecks, or underserved populations.
5. Detail priority areas for new policy intervention.

### Structured Gap Analysis Output Format
Provide a formal report containing:
- **Executive Summary**: Overview of the issue and findings.
- **Existing Frameworks & Schemes**: List of active central and state schemes.
- **Identified Gaps**: Detailed analysis of why current schemes are failing or inadequate for this specific problem.
- **Target Beneficiary Analysis**: Description of who is left behind (underserved cohorts).
- **Intervention Priorities**: Specific, actionable areas that a new policy must address.
